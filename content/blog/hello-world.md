# Welcome to My Blog

This is my first blog post on my new Wikipedia-style portfolio website. I'm excited to share my thoughts, experiences, and insights about web development, design, and technology.

## What to Expect

On this blog, you'll find articles about:

- **Web Development**: Tips, tutorials, and best practices for building modern web applications
- **Design**: Thoughts on UI/UX design, accessibility, and creating delightful user experiences
- **Technology**: Explorations of new tools, frameworks, and emerging technologies
- **Career**: Reflections on my journey as a developer and lessons learned along the way

## Why Wikipedia Style?

I chose to build my portfolio in a Wikipedia-inspired style because I believe in the power of clean, content-focused design. Wikipedia's interface is:

1. **Accessible** - Easy to read and navigate
2. **Timeless** - The design has remained effective for decades
3. **Content-First** - The focus is always on the information
